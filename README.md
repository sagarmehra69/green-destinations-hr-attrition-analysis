# 🌿 Green Destinations HR Attrition Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
Green Destinations, a travel agency, has experienced rising employee attrition. This project analyzes HR data to identify patterns and key factors influencing employee turnover.

The goal is to help HR understand:
- What is the attrition rate?
- Which factors influence employees leaving?

## 🎯 Objectives
- Calculate overall attrition rate
- Identify trends based on:
  - Age
  - Years at Company
  - Income
- Analyze impact of:
  - Job Role
  - Overtime
  - Work-Life Balance
  - Gender & Marital Status

---

- Attrition by Age Group

- Department-wise Attrition
- Overtime Impact
- Gender Distribution
- Marital Status Analysis
- Work-Life Balance Heatmap

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Highest attrition observed among employees with **0–2 years tenure**
- Younger employees (Under 25) show higher attrition rates
- Employees with **low income** are more likely to leave
- **Sales Representatives** have the highest attrition among job roles
- Overtime significantly increases attrition risk
